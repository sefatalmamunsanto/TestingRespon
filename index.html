Pi Lockup Manager
Real Blockchain Integration
This application connects to the Pi Network blockchain to fetch real wallet lockup balances. No seed phrases are stored or transmitted.

Understanding Lockup

A digital asset lockup is when your Pi tokens are held in a time-locked state, preventing withdrawal until a specified date. This ensures long-term commitment in blockchain projects.

Early Unlock Option

You can request to unlock your locked tokens before maturity. However, this early release attracts a 3% service fee, which is deducted automatically from your locked balance.

Fee Breakdown

Service Fee 3%

The 3% fee incentivizes holding until the natural maturity date. This is automatically deducted from your locked balance.
